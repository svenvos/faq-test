FAQ

  • What is your return policy?

    We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.

  • How long does shipping take?

    Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 2-3 business days.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary based on location.

  • Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

    Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours. After that, they are processed for shipping.

  • How can I contact customer support?

    You can reach us via email at support@example.com or through our live chat during business hours.
